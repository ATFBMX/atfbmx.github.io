Who We Are

We are a community of BMX flatland riders based in Toronto. We connect riders, run meetups and jams, and promote flatland culture across Canada.

What We Do

  • Open practice sessions & rider mentoring
  • Local jams, demos & community events
  • Beginner support: tips, setup, and progression
